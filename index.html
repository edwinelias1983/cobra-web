Before anything else, we start exactly here.
COBRA uses your existing symbols — the things that already make sense to you — to explain new ideas in a way that fits your lived reality. Voice helps COBRA match tone, rhythm, and explanation style so learning feels natural, not forced. 


    

      COBRA does not ask for abstract parameters.

      Domain, phase, and symbols are inferred internally from your answers —
      so learning starts from your world, not a preset system.
